Help And Support

Welcome to the Help and Support page for our Note Keeper App. We're here to assist you in making the most out of our application. Whether you're new to note-taking or simply need a refresher on how to use our features, this page provides guidance on common tasks and answers to frequently asked questions.


Getting Started

  1. Creating a New Note

    2. To create a new note, follow these steps:

    • Click on the "Title" input field and type the title of your note.
    • In the "Take a note..." text area, add the content you want for your note.
    • Click the "Add" button with a plus icon to save your new note.

  2. Editing a Note

    3. If you want to edit an existing note, you can:

    • Hover over the note you wish to edit.
    • Click on the pencil icon that appears on the top right of the note.
    • Update the title and content as needed.
    • Click the "Save" button to confirm your changes.

  3. Deleting a Note

    4. To remove a note, do the following:

    • Hover over the note you want to delete.
    • Click on the trash can icon that appears on the bottom right of the note.
    • Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Searching for Notes

  1. Search Functionality

    2. We've implemented a search feature to help you quickly find specific notes. To use it:

    • Type your search query in the "Search" bar at the top of the page.
    • As you type, the notes list will automatically update to display only those notes that match your search terms.

  2. Read More

    3. If a note has more content than what is initially displayed, you can click on the "Read More" button at the bottom of the note to view the full content in a modal window.


Troubleshooting

  1. Technical Issues

    2. If you encounter technical issues or errors while using the app, we recommend:

    • Refreshing the page to see if the problem resolves itself.
    • Checking your internet connection.
    • Clearing your browser's cache and cookies, which can sometimes resolve display problems.

  2. Lost Notes

    3. If your notes have disappeared or are not loading, please ensure that you're using the same device and browser you used when you created the notes. If you're still experiencing issues, you can reach out to our support team for assistance.

Contact Us

  • If you have questions or need further assistance that isn't covered in this help page, please don't hesitate to contact our support team. You can reach us by email at Contact us.
